Settings

Manage your profile and privacy on this device.

Profile

Only your display name and avatar are public to other Relay users.

Photos are compressed locally before saving.

Security

Privacy lock

Automatically logs this device out after 30 idle minutes.

Relay never stores plain-text passwords. Your password is hashed in the browser with PBKDF2 and a unique salt. For production, pair this UI with Firebase Authentication and the Firestore rules in README.md.

Danger Zone

Deleting your local account removes it from this browser and removes your public profile from Firestore when configured.
